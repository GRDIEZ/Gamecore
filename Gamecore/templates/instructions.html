How do you schedule the games?

GameCore schedules the games with you and your opponent based on the availability that you have provided under ’My Availability’ in your account

How does GameCore receive the scores?

The home player takes a picture of the results screen and uploads that on the GameCore platform or sends that to Scores@GameCorePro.com

How do I setup the games?

Prior to each game, you will receive your opponent’s PSN ID and all the details for the game. The home team then sets up the game and invites the other player
